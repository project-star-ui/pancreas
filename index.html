Meet Your Pancreas!

The sugar-monster inside your belly

What's a Pancreas?

Your pancreas is like a tiny factory inside your body that helps you digest food and control sugar levels!

It's about 6 inches long and hides behind your stomach, working hard even when you're sleeping.

Pancreas Location

Your pancreas sits in the middle of your belly, behind your stomach and in front of your spine.

It's shaped like a floppy fish with its head tucked into the curve of your small intestine!

What Does the Pancreas Do?

Enzyme Factory

The pancreas makes special juices called enzymes that help break down your food into tiny pieces your body can use.

Carb
Protein
Fat

Sugar Manager

It makes insulin and glucagon, special messengers that tell your body how to use sugar for energy.

Fun Pancreas Facts!

Double Duty

Your pancreas has two jobs: help digestion AND control blood sugar. That's like being both a chef and a traffic cop!

Quiet Worker

The pancreas does its job so well you never feel it working. It only speaks up when something's wrong.

Size Matters

A kid's pancreas is about the size of a small banana, while an adult's is like a large banana!

How to Keep Your Pancreas Happy

  • Eat colorful fruits and veggies - they help your pancreas work better!
  • Drink plenty of water - it helps all your body parts, including the pancreas!
  • Exercise daily - moving helps your insulin work better!
  • Limit sugary treats - too much sugar makes your pancreas work too hard!
